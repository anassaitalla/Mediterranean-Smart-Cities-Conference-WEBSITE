Smart City Hackathon

Smart Cities Hackathon is open students of Universities and engineering & Business schools who would like to contribute to make their city a better place through Smart Cities innovative applications and solutions. This Hackathon will take place on May 2-4, 2024 and it is a completely free event in which competing university students teams wishing to make a difference in their cities through innovative smart cities solutions are welcome ! All participating teams will have the chance to present their projects in a rocket pitch fashion to the judges, who will decide the winners.

WHERE, WHEN AND HOW?

  • May 2-4, 2024
  • Location: ENS, Martil, Morocco
  • Teams must have at least 3 participants and a maximum of 5
  • Projects should follow the guidelines indicated in this link: https://med-sc.org/

WHAT SHOULD YOU CREATE?

The Smart Cities Hackathon participants are required to build innovative solutions for a Smarter Cities through a rapid pace development. These solutions should include a functional prototype related to one of the areas below.

SMART CITIES AREAS

  • Transportation and Mobility
  • Smart Governance
  • Education
  • Social Inclusion
  • Tourism and Cultural Heritage
  • Economic Development
  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Energy Efficiency
  • Urban Planning
  • Waste Management
  • Healthcare
  • Public Safety and Security

EVALUATION RUBRIC

*Challenges and evaluation criteria may change according to the event's organizing committee
