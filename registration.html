TERMS AND POLICIES

  • All conference participants must be registered;
  • Only registered authors can participate in conferences and present their papers;
  • If the registered author is absent, another co-author in his/her status can replace it;
  • It is mandatory for at least one author of an accepted paper to register and present so that it can be published in the Proceedings and included in the Technical Program;
  • Students must provide proof of their status with a copy of their student card sent or a copy of their Certificate from the Head of the College or University with the registration form.
  • The participants who are members of the IEEE should attach proof of membership.

ADDITIONAL ITEMS

  • Authors with more than one accepted paper must pay only an "Extra Paper/Publication Charge" of 60 € for each extra paper.
  • Above 6 pages in the given IEEE Format, there will be extra page charges of 30 € for each one page.
  • Ticket for the gala dinner can be purchased at 30 €.

PRESENTATION MODES

In case of hesitation by some authors/speakers to travel during Year 2024 (due to Coronavirus/COVID-19 or other pandemics), the MSCC Organizing Committee has developed the following policy in regards to presentation modes:

If an author of an accepted paper registers for the conference but for some reason he/she is unable to physically attend the conference: The paper of the registered author would still be published, and arrangements would be made for the published proceedings to be made available to the author after the conference. Therefore, the non-attendance by the author would not negatively impact the publication of his/her paper.

Registered authors are requested to select one of the presentation options:

  1. Presenting the paper on site (face-to-face) at the conference
  2. Presenting the paper remotely: using Zoom (interactive)
