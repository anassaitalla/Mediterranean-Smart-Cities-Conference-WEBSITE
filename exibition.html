Exhibition

SEE THE FUTURE OF SMART CITIES
A SPACE FOR NETWORKING AND VISIBILITY
JOIN OUR FIRST EXPO!

     Smart City Expo will offer an industrial and technological showcase of the Smart City ecosystem worldwide. The exhibition space aims to attract a wide range of professionals, innovators, key industry leading innovators, and stakeholders from the government and intergovernmental organizations (Local Authorities, State Governments, Public Administration), Institutions (public, private, non-profit, local institutions), the academic sector (Universities, think tanks, research and development centers), international companies, SMEs, startups, media, etc. It's an ideal channel for those who want to showcase their products or services to visitors, exhibitors, organizers of the MSCC 2024, partners, and professionals in the Smart Cities development sector. This area is designed to accommodate a large number of visitors, which will enable companies to gain significant visibility.
     The necessary services and facilities for the smooth running of this space will facilitate the connection of these companies with potential partners, city delegations, institutional representatives, and experts. All attendees of the exhibition will also benefit from a dedicated innovation agora hosting startups presenting initiatives, projects, and innovations aimed at improving the quality of life through the development of Smart Cities.
