Tutorial 1: An Example of Real Smart Grid: use of Dynamic Line Ratings to Connect more Renewables (Tutorial)

SmartGrids is an overused buzz word covering all sort of applications enable grids to be operated in a more automated way. Ampacimon offers a range of “real smart” applications, one of them being the use of sensors on high voltage transmission and distribution line to maximize their thermal capacity in order to alleviate congestions. Dynamic Line Rating is a technology making use of the fact that overhead lines are cooled down by prevailing weather conditions, which, if measured properly and translated into meaningful information flowing all the way to the dispatching room, enable grid operators to carry typically 30% more energy on their lines. One specific use case is the use of DLR to quickly integrate more renewable generation, at low cost, and, even more importantly, within months, not years.
